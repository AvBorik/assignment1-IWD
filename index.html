Introduction to Queenstown

Queenstown (Māori: Tāhuna) is the best plase to visit. The city is placed in the south island in Otago region of New Zealand. Queenstown is a resort town and there are many places for tourists to visit. The population of Queenstown is 14,300 (June 2016) and it is ranking 27th city in New Zealand population rating and second large city in Otago. Queenstown is placed on Queenstown bay of lake Wakatipu. There are lots of mountains placed around the city, such as The Remarkables, Cecil Peak, Walter Peak. Also, Ben Lomond and Queenstown Hill located close to the city. A great view opens up on these mountains and hills which makes this city very popular amongst tourists. Queenstown is also located near Dunedin city which is largest city in Otago region. The main speciality of the Queenstown is commerce-oriented tourism, especially adventure and ski tourism.
